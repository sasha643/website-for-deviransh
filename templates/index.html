Our Gypsum-Based Technical Boards, sourced from industry leaders USG Knauf and Gyproc, provide innovative building alternatives. Resistant to fire, moisture, impact, sound, and more, these boards offer rock-solid durability for a wide range of construction challenges.

Deviransh Enterprise proudly presents top-quality Gypsum Boards sourced from renowned manufacturers such as USG Knauf and Gyproc. These boards serve as the foundation for sturdy and versatile interior applications, offering a perfect blend of durability and design flexibility.
